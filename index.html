Our Story

In June 2023, we decided to build a platform where creativity, freedom of expression, and light are shared, through photography and storytelling. A secure place where the seeds of knowledge are nurtured through art and appreciation; a group where kindness is encouraged and differences honored. Please share a pear today and help us extend the branches of a true online community devoted to the exchange of experiences celebrating the human connection.
